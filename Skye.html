"Skye", also known as Kirra Foster, is a radiant
from Australia. Her alias is "The great reclaimer".
She is the 14th recruit of the Valorant Protocol.


Skye is an INITIATOR. Other INITIATOR Agents in the protocol include Breach, Fade, KAY/O, and Sova.

The abilities of an Initiator are mainly used to gather information and to flush people out of their positions.
