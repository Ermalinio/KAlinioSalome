Tricks

1. Double Slide When activating her ultimate, she regains a slide. Using this knowledge, one can slide twice immediately after one another if they were to activate their ultimate in the middle of the first slide's animation.
2. Neon Updraft Using the head-hitbox of a fellow teamate, dash towards them at a specific elevated angle from them. This will create an updraft(gain incredible height) like motion with slight horizontal velocity letting you reach very high places. It is recommended to do this pre-round as the dash recharges.
